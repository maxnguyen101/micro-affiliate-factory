# Cluster #3 Research Shortlist (Low-Risk, High-Value Hybrid)

Scoring model (1-5 each):
- Payout potential (SaaS recurring + compatible high-ticket accessory layer)
- Buyer intent clarity (problem-aware, solution-seeking)
- Compliance safety (non-YMYL, operational/productivity framing)
- Tool utility fit (can support an interactive picker)

## 1) Help Desk + Live Chat + AI Inbox Triage Stack (**Winner**) 
- Example SaaS vendors with partner programs: Intercom, Freshworks/Freshdesk, Zendesk ecosystem, Help Scout
- Accessory layer: USB headsets, webcams, second monitors, docking peripherals
- Payout: 4 | Intent: 5 | Compliance safety: 5 | Tool fit: 5 | **Total: 19**

Why it wins:
- Clear commercial intent from support managers and CX leads comparing response-time workflows.
- Low compliance risk (ops tooling; no medical/legal/finance advice).
- Strong hybrid angle: software stack + practical support-desk hardware bundle.
