# Power Cluster Plan — AI Sales Call Recording & Conversation Intelligence SaaS

## Cluster Goal
Help B2B teams pick the right call intelligence stack quickly using criteria-based comparisons and one decision micro-tool.

## Audience Segments
- Founder-led sales teams (1-10 reps)
- SDR/AE managers (10-50 reps)
- Agencies managing outbound for clients
- RevOps implementers comparing stack fit

## Internal Linking Map
- Tool page links to all money pages by scenario output.
- Each money page links to:
  - Tool page
  - Relevant support pages (setup, mistakes, glossary, troubleshooting)
- Support pages link back to one primary money page + tool.

## Pages in this Cluster

### 1) Micro-Tool
- `sales-call-intelligence-picker` (interactive selector)

### 2) Money Pages (3)
1. Best AI Sales Call Recording Tools for SMB B2B Teams
2. Gong vs Chorus vs Avoma (criteria comparison)
3. Best Call Intelligence Tools for Agencies Managing Client Outbound

### 3) Support Pages (5)
1. How to choose call recording software without overbuying
2. 7 mistakes teams make when adopting conversation intelligence
3. CRM compatibility checklist (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
4. Call scoring glossary for sales managers
5. Troubleshooting poor transcript quality and adoption issues

## Money Page Template Requirements
- Affiliate disclosure block appears above first outbound affiliate link.
- “Pick in 10 seconds” quick recommendation section.
- Decision table with criteria (team size, CRM, budget style, coaching depth, onboarding effort).
- No hard price figures unless compliant approved source flow is enabled; default = no prices.

## Funnel Logic
- TOFU support content -> tool -> money page -> outbound affiliate click.
